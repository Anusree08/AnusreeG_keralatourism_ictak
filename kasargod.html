Kasargod

Kasaragod is the northernmost district of Kerala and is included in the North Malabar region. It is also known as Saptha Bhasha Sangama Bhoomi (The land of seven languages), as seven languages namely Malayalam, Tulu, Kannada, Marathi, Konkani, Beary, and Urdu are spoken, unlike the other districts of Kerala. It was a part of the Kannur district of Kerala until 24 May 1984. Kasaragod district has the maximum number of rivers in Kerala-12
